🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

B-TALK is committed to protecting your privacy.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

  • Google Account info (name, email, profile photo) — via Google Login
  • Firebase UID — to identify your account
  • Voice data — processed in real time for conversation; not stored permanently, except as described below under "Reported Call Recordings"
  • Reported call recordings — when a user submits a report during a call, a short audio clip (up to 30 seconds) is automatically recorded. This clip is used solely for moderation review and is deleted within 30 days of the report being resolved, unless retention is required for legal compliance.
  • IP address — collected automatically by our server infrastructure
  • Inquiry messages — stored in Firebase when you contact us
  • Usage data — session timestamps, channel activity, report and moderation history

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To provide and operate the B-TALK service
  • To match you with other users by topic
  • To enforce our Community Guidelines and Terms of Service
  • To review reported conversations and take moderation action
  • To respond to inquiries and appeals

FIREBASE & GOOGLE

We use Firebase (Google LLC) for authentication, real-time database, and hosting. Your data may be processed on Google's servers. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

COOKIES

We use minimal cookies for session management and language preferences. No advertising cookies are used.

DATA RETENTION

Account data is retained until you delete your account. Inquiry messages are retained for support purposes. Reported call recordings are retained only for the duration of the moderation and appeal process, then deleted.

YOUR RIGHTS

You may request deletion of your data, or request removal of a report from your record via a successful appeal, by using the in-app Contact form.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY / AGE REQUIREMENT

B-TALK is intended solely for users aged 18 and over and is not directed at minors. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a user under 18 has created an account, we will take steps to delete the account and associated information promptly.

CONTACT

Use the Contact form available in the app menu.
